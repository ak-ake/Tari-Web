About Tari

Tari is a 3d open world game where you'll survive in a world of Dead Souls.
Our mission is to create a unique gaming experience that combines creativity, survival, and storytelling in a captivating open world environment.
Join us on this exciting journey as we bring Tari to life!


Our Journey

Tari project idea was made by Akib (Ake) and Kousik (Echo) in 2024.

Because of Illness of Akib the game development was stopped and a tragedy happened, our saved SSD storage got corrupted.

Later, the project was revived and continued with renewed determination.

We Are still working but because of limited resource we are facing problem while developing so we're restarting it from scratch and This game will be written in C++.

Let's dive into it we'll inform you regularly.
